Inside — Modes · Features · Specifications

Operating positions

Three modes.
One handle.

All three positions accessible with a single espagnolette handle. No secondary hardware required. Tilt-turn hardware certified to EN 13126.

01 · Closed

Sealed tight.

Multi-point lock engaged at all six perimeter positions. Full compression seal. Maximum thermal and acoustic performance.

02 · Tilt

Secure ventilation.

Top tilts inward on a base hinge. Fresh air enters without compromising weather resistance or security.

03 · Turn

Full access.

Side opens fully inward on a vertical stile hinge. Maximum airflow and access for cleaning.

Performance you can feel

Engineered from the inside out.

Thermal break

The 34 mm polyamide strip separates inner and outer aluminum profiles. Heat stays inside in winter and outside in summer. U-value ≤ 1.4 W/m²K.

Multi-point locking

Six lock points engage around the sash perimeter on a single handle turn. Certified EN 1627 burglar resistance.

Acoustic insulation

Profile geometry and triple-compression seals reduce airborne noise. Traffic, wind, and infiltration held outside the frame.

Weather resistance

Compression seals on all three sides. Certified at 600 Pa over 48 hours of wind-driven rain. Tested, not implied.

Corrosion resistance

Anodised and powder-coated aluminum profiles. Anthracite RAL 7016. Rated for coastal and high-humidity environments over decades.

Profile system

6500 RPT geometry optimises drainage, seal compression, and thermal continuity simultaneously. European engineered.
